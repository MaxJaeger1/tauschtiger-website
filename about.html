Our Story

How we're making sustainable fashion for kids both easy and joyful.

From a Parent's Frustration to a Movement

TauschTiger was born in a small apartment in Berlin, where a parent stared at a mountain of outgrown baby clothes and thought: "There has to be a better way."

Kids' clothes are worn for mere months before they're outgrown. Buying new constantly felt wasteful. Storing hand-me-downs felt chaotic. Donating felt... insufficient. We knew millions of families faced the same dilemma.

So we asked: What if we built a system where clothes cycle endlessly? Where kids always have fresh, curated outfits. Where parents never feel guilty. Where sustainability feels *easy*, not like a sacrifice.

TauschTiger was that answer. "They grow. We swap." became our north star.

How We Calculate Impact

We partner with sustainability scientists to measure every aspect: water consumption, carbon emissions, landfill diversion, and ethical labor standards. Every returned item is tracked and either resold, repurposed, or recycled responsibly. Zero items end up in landfills.
